A new complication in the CODE 11.59 by Audemars Piguet :

GLASS

The Manufacture has created a contemporary double curved sapphire crystal, whose tense, arched profile plays with depth, perspective and light for a unique optic experience. While its internal surface is shaped like a dome, it is vertically curved from 6 to 12 o’clock.

CASE

18-carat white gold case, double glareproofed sapphire crystal and caseback.

SIZE : 41 mm

THICKNESS : 13.8 mm

WATER RESISTANCE : 30 m

DIAL

Lacquered blue inner bezel, openworked dial, 18-carat white gold hands.

BRACELET

Hand-stitched “large square scale” blue alligator strap with 18-carat white gold AP folding clasp.
